# Gulf + Middle East Signals

Public **examples** of the Gulf + Middle East Hybrid Intelligence Skill style. Each signal shows how the skill turns one regional event or structural condition into a bounded, decision-aware note.

## What this archive is

- a public set of example outputs from the skill;
- a way to see the memo style at signal-length scale;
- a starting point for adapting the skill to your own internal signals.

## What this archive is not

- not official intelligence;
- not legal, compliance, sanctions, AML, vessel screening, or investment advice;
- not a guarantee of factual verification beyond what each signal explicitly cites;
- not real-time. Signals are dated and may be stale; verify primary sources before use.

## Contributing a signal

Copy [`TEMPLATE.md`](TEMPLATE.md) to `signals/YYYY/YYYY-MM-DD-<topic>.md`, fill in every section including evidence mode and disclaimer, and open a pull request.

Reviewers check:
- evidence mode is stated and content respects it;
- Iran actor distinction used where Iran is relevant;
- GCC actor distinction used where GCC actors are material;
- no fabricated citations, vessel names, IMO numbers, or sanctions designations;
- limitation note included.
